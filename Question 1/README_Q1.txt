1)Please install the necessary modules before running the program.

2)The program checks if the input year given by user is a leap year. If yes, then it displays the day of the extra in the year. IF the entered year is not a leap year then it finds the closest leap year either before or after the current year and then print the day of extra day in the year.
In case of closest leap year before and after the current year is equidistant from the current year, it prints the day of extra day in both cases.
